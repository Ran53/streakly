Streakly Privacy Policy

App Name: Streakly
Last updated: 12 DECEMBER 2025

Introduction

We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our app. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share your information.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information:
We only collect your name and device ID to help us maintain your account.

1.2 Health Information:
We may collect information related to your mental health, such as responses to mental health tests, mood tracking data, and interactions with our therapy chatbot.

1.3 Usage Data:
We collect information about your interactions with our app, such as the features you use, the time and duration of your sessions, and other usage statistics.

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 To Provide and Improve Services:
We use your information to deliver, maintain, and improve our services, personalize your experience, and develop new features.

2.2 To Communicate with You:
We use your information to send you updates, newsletters, and other communications related to your use of our app.

2.3 For Research and Analytics:
We use your information to understand how our services are used and to evaluate and improve our app's performance.

3. Sharing Your Information

3.1 Service Providers:
We DO NOT share your information with third-party service providers who perform services on our behalf, such as hosting, data analysis, and customer support.

3.2 Legal Compliance:
We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

3.3 Business Transfers:
In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or a portion of our assets, your information may be transferred to the acquiring entity.

4. Your Choices

4.1 Access and Correction:
You have the right to access and correct your personal information. You can update your information through your account settings.

4.2 Data Retention:
We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy.

4.3 Opt-Out:
You can opt out of receiving promotional communications from us by following the instructions in those communications or by contacting us directly.

5. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no internet or email transmission is ever fully secure or error-free.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on our app and updating the "Last updated" date.
